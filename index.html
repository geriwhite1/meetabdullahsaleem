Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common digital marketing questions

How is digital marketing different from traditional marketing?

Digital marketing focuses exclusively on online channels (websites, social media, search engines, email) and offers precise targeting, real-time analytics, and measurable ROI. Unlike traditional marketing, it allows for continuous optimization based on performance data.

How long does it take to see results from digital marketing?

Paid campaigns can show immediate results, while SEO typically takes 3-6 months to gain traction. A comprehensive strategy usually shows measurable impact within the first 30-60 days, with compounding results over time.

What metrics do you track to measure success?

I focus on business-impacting metrics: conversion rates, customer acquisition cost, lifetime value, ROI, and revenue attribution. We also track channel-specific KPIs like organic traffic, click-through rates, and engagement metrics.

Do you work with businesses in any industry?

While I have experience across multiple industries, I specialize in e-commerce, SaaS, professional services, and technology companies. During our discovery call, I'll assess if my expertise aligns with your specific needs.

What makes your approach different?

I combine data analytics with creative strategy, focusing on sustainable growth rather than quick wins. My methodology integrates all digital channels into a cohesive system that drives compounding results over time.
